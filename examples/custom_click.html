jQuery imgViewer

Custom Click Callback Example

Download Download View imgViewer on GitHub

This example demonstrates the widget with a custom "onClick" callback. In this case clicking on the image will show both the page pixel coordinates and relative image coordinates (relative image coordinates range from 0,0 to 1,1 where 0,0 is the top-left corner and 1,1 is the bottom-right corner of the image).

Zoom in and out using the mousewheel. Left mouseclick and drag to pan or use touch gestures (pinch to zoom and tap and drag to pan). Resize the window and try again.
