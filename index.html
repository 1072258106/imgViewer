imgViewer

jQuery plugin to zoom and pan images

Download Download View imgViewer on GitHub

imgViewer is a jQuery plugin that adds to an image the ability to zoom in and out with the mousewheel and pan around by click and drag. A unique feature of this plugin is it's ability to work on images which have widths or heights specified as a percentage of their container.
